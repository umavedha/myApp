kapaleeswarar temple

The Kapaleeshwarar Temple in Chennai is one of the popular temples in Tamil Nadu. The 7th century Arulmigu Kapaleeswarar temple is dedicated to Goddess Parvati in the form of Karpagambal or the Goddess of the Wish-Yielding Tree and Lord Shiva.
